why zcash

Privacy isn't a feature — it's the foundation

🔒

Shielded by default

Z-addresses use zk-SNARKs to hide sender, receiver, and amount. Supporters tip you without exposing their wallet history.

💬

Encrypted memos

Supporters can leave you a private note alongside their tip. Only you, the recipient, can read it — it lives inside the shielded transaction.

🌐

No middleman

Unlike Ko-fi or Patreon, ZecTip is purely client-side. No accounts, no fees, no platform risk. Your funds go straight to your wallet.

📦

Embeds anywhere

HTML, Ghost, Substack, personal portfolio — if it accepts HTML, it accepts your ZecTip button. One snippet, infinite placements.

📱

QR-first UX

The ZEC URI standard is supported by all major Zcash wallets. Scan, confirm, done — no copy-pasting long addresses.

⚡

Open source

Built for a university blockchain class. All code is open, auditable, and free to fork. Contribute on GitHub.
